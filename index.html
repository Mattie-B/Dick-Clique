Dick Grayson Fan Site

Richard "Dick" John Grayson, is an acrobat trained from birth in the trapeze act the Flying Grayson's at Haly's Circus. Dick is a half Romaini and half White, 5'10 male, living in the city of Bludhaven. Dick Grayson was created in April 1940 in Detective Comics #38 as the first, Robin, the Boy Wonder. Robin would work with Batman for many years and go on many adventures with him as well as a team of superheroes called the Teen Titans. Dick would eventually strike out on his own, but when his team is in distress, he comes out of retierment and adopts the new identity of Nightwing in Tales of the Teen Titans #43 in 1984 by Marv Wolfman and George Perez. Nightwing would later have his own city to protect called Bludhaven and slowly grows into his own hero.
