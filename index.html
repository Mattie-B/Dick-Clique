Dick Grayson Fan Site

Richard "Dick" John Grayson, is an acrobat trained from birth in the trapeze act the Flying Grayson's at Haly's Circus. Dick is a half Romaini and half White, 5'10 male, living in the city of Bludhaven. Dick Grayson was created in April 1940 in Detective Comics #38 as the first, Robin, the Boy Wonder. Robin would work with Batman for many years and go on many adventures with him as well as a team of superheroes called the Teen Titans. Dick would eventually strike out on his own, but when his team is in distress, he comes out of retierment and adopts the new identity of Nightwing in Tales of the Teen Titans #43 in 1984 by Marv Wolfman and George Perez. Dick Grayson has gone by many aliases over the years such as Robin, Red Robin, Renegade, Red X, The Target, Batman, Agent 37, but his most iconic by far is the hero of Bludhaven "Nightwing." Nightwing would later have his own city to protect called Bludhaven and slowly grows into his own hero.

With the character of Dick Grayson being 80+ years old, it could seem intimidating to jump into the story of such and iconic and recognizable character.
